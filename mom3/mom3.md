## Retirement

The minimum retirement age is 63 years.

In accordance with the Retirement and Re-employment Act from 1 July 2022, the minimum retirement age is **63 years**. Your company cannot ask you to retire before that age.

You have this protection if you:
*   Are a Singapore Citizen or Permanent Resident.
*   Have worked for your employer for at least 3 months.

Employees who turn 63 can continue to be employed in the organisation if they meet the re-employment eligibility criteria.

The retirement age will be raised to 64 years from 1 July 2026. It will be applicable to those born on or after 1 July 1963.

### When do I retire

You will retire on the day you reach the minimum retirement age.

*   If you turn 63 on 1 August 2022, your retirement date will be 1 August 2022.

### Wrongful dismissal

If you feel you have been wrongfully dismissed because of your age, you can [file a claim for wrongful dismissal](https://www.mom.gov.sg/employment-practices/termination-of-employment/wrongful-dismissal) within **1 month** of dismissal.
