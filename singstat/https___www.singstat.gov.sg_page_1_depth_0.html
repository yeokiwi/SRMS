Official website links end with .gov.sg

Secure websites use HTTPS

Look for a lock () or https:// as an added precaution. Share sensitive information only on official, secure websites.

Economy and Prices

21 Nov 2025
The Singapore economy expanded by 4.2% on a year-on-year basis in 3Q 2025. The GDP growth forecast for 2025 has been upgraded from 1.5% to 2.5% to around 4.0%. For 2026, Singapores GDP growth is projected to come in at 1.0% to 3.0%.

Domestic wholesale sales increased 0.6% in 3Q 2025 compared to 3Q 2024. Excluding petroleum, domestic wholesale sales fell 2.1%. Foreign wholesale sales fell 5.1% over the same period. Excluding petroleum, foreign wholesale sales grew by 2.8%.
