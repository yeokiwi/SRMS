## Latest Highlights
PreviousNext
###  Industry
05 Dec 2025
Retail sales increased 4.5% in Oct 2025 compared to Oct 2024. Excluding motor vehicles, retail sales rose 3.7%. Sales of food & beverage services rose 2.4% in Oct 2025 compared to Oct 2024.
###  Economy and Prices
28 Nov 2025
The Singapore Manufactured Products and Domestic Supply Price indices rose 1.2% and 0.3% respectively in Oct 2025 over Sep 2025. In contrast, the Import Price Index fell 0.7% and the Export Price Index was unchanged.
###  Industry
27 Nov 2025
Business receipts of Singapore’s Services industries, excluding Wholesale Trade, Retail Trade, Accommodation and Food Services, increased 5.3% in 3Q 2025 compared to 3Q 2024.
###  Economy and Prices
27 Nov 2025
In 2023, Cross-border Supply was the predominant mode of supply, constituting 82.6% and 72.4% of total services exports and imports respectively, while Consumption Abroad and Presence of Natural Persons contributed to the remainder.
###  Industry
26 Nov 2025
Manufacturing output increased 11.5% in Oct 2025 on a seasonally adjusted month-on-month basis.
###  Economy and Prices
25 Nov 2025
In nominal terms, personal disposable income rose 2.8% in 3Q 2025, moderating from the 3.6% increase in the previous quarter. Personal saving rate increased to 33.5% in 3Q 2025 from 30.6% in the previous quarter.
###  Economy and Prices
25 Nov 2025
Household net worth rose 8.9% on a year-on-year basis in 3Q 2025. Growth in assets and liabilities increased in 3Q 2025 compared to 2Q 2025.
###  Economy and Prices
24 Nov 2025
CPI in Oct 2025 was unchanged from the previous month and rose 1.2% from a year earlier.
###  Economy and Prices
24 Nov 2025
Joint press release on “Consumer Price Developments in Oct 2025” by MAS and MTI.
###  Economy and Prices
21 Nov 2025
The Singapore economy expanded by 4.2% on a year-on-year basis in 3Q 2025. The GDP growth forecast for 2025 has been upgraded from ‘1.5% to 2.5%’ to ‘around 4.0%’. For 2026, Singapore’s GDP growth is projected to come in at ‘1.0% to 3.0%’.
###  Industry
20 Nov 2025
Domestic wholesale sales increased 0.6% in 3Q 2025 compared to 3Q 2024. Excluding petroleum, domestic wholesale sales fell 2.1%. Foreign wholesale sales fell 5.1% over the same period. Excluding petroleum, foreign wholesale sales grew by 2.8%.
###  Trade and Investment
17 Nov 2025
Non-oil domestic exports expanded by 22.2% in Oct 2025 on a year-on-year basis, following the 7.0% increase in the previous month.
